About Linus & Chester
Linus and Chester have been inseparable since puppyhood — they sleep curled together, greet the day together, and have never spent a night apart. Linus is the social butterfly of the pair, always eager for a gentle pat or a slow walk around the block. Chester is the thinker: he'll quietly watch the world go by from his favourite window spot, occasionally nudging Linus to confirm everything is okay.

Both boys are fully house-trained, remarkably calm, and genuinely low-maintenance. They'd thrive in a quiet apartment with an adult household or a calm senior who enjoys steady, unhurried companionship. They've been at the shelter for 34 days and are ready to go home — together, forever.
