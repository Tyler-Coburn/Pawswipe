About BC SPCA Vancouver
The BC SPCA is British Columbia's oldest and most trusted animal welfare organisation — and the only one with the authority to investigate and enforce animal cruelty laws under the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act. Since 1895, the Vancouver branch has been a cornerstone of the city's relationship with animals, connecting thousands of pets with loving families each year.

The Vancouver location handles dogs, cats, rabbits, small animals, and occasional exotic species. The site also includes the Dr. Doug Ingstrup Animal Hospital, a non-profit veterinary clinic open to the public — providing quality care at accessible prices. All adoption fees help fund ongoing shelter operations, medical care, and community outreach programs across BC.
