### Sending tips
- Send Tuesday–Thursday mornings; shelters are busiest on weekends.
- One follow-up after ~5 business days is fine; then leave it.
- Lead with *their* animals (you already have real names) — it shows you did the work.
- Offer the demo link read-only; don't ask for anything in the first email except a short call.
