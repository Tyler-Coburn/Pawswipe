The Process

Three steps to forever friends

01 🏠
Build your profile

Tell us about your home, lifestyle, activity level, and what you're looking for. Our system uses this to surface truly compatible matches — not just cute faces.

02 ✨
Swipe & connect

Browse pet cards tuned to your life. Swipe right to express interest, super-like urgent rescues, and save favorites to revisit. Every swipe teaches the system.

03 🎉
Meet & adopt

Once matched, message the shelter directly, schedule a virtual meet-and-greet, and complete the adoption — all without leaving the app.

AI Compatibility Matching

Our behavioral engine goes beyond breed filters. It maps your lifestyle — work schedule, activity level, housing, household members — to a pet's actual temperament. A high-energy husky won't appear for a studio apartment dweller who works 12-hour days. Smarter matches mean fewer returns and happier animals.
